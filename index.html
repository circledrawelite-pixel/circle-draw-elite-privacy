Privacy Policy for Circle Draw Elite

Last Updated: June 2026

Circle Draw Elite respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user information.

Information We Collect

Circle Draw Elite does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Local Device Storage

The game stores gameplay information locally on your device, including:

  • XP and progression
  • Coins and rewards
  • Unlocked themes
  • Game settings and preferences

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Internet Access

The application may use internet access for platform services and future updates. No personal information is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Circle Draw Elite currently does not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Cloud save services
  • User account systems

Children's Privacy

Circle Draw Elite does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
